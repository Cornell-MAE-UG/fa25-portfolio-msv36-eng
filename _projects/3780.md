# My Contributions
For this project, my contribution varied based on the stage we were at in project. For the proposal, I created our slideshow presentation. I helped build and wire the robots, along with set up the QTI Sensor. On competition day, I was in charge of keeping track of the rounds to make sure we were on time and fixing any broken parts between rounds such as the rubber bands.
